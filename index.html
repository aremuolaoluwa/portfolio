About me

I have professional experience in Computing Education and Educational Technology. My interest lies in the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Educational Technology.

I provide computing education to individuals and students, especially those from underserved communities. My major task has been on equipping students from underrepresented areas with digital skills.

As a computer science educator and UI/UX designer, I am passionate about developing technology-driven tools to enhance teaching methods and improve learning outcomes. I am also passionate about how early exposure to computing education can improve students' learning outcomes.
